Slow Cooker White Wine Chicken Stew
Prep: 20m    Cook Time: 7hr 15m    Total Time: 7hr 35m    Serves: 4

Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 1/2 pounds boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1 inch pieces
Salt and pepper
2 cups chicken broth
1/2 cup dry white wine
1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
2 teaspoons chopped fresh rosemary
2 teaspoons chopped fresh thyme
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
1 teaspoon salt
Lots ground black pepper
6 garlic cloves, finely minced
1 large yellow onion, cut into chunks
3 large carrots, peeled and cut into 1/2-inch diagonal slices
1 pound yukon gold potatoes, diced into 1/2 inch cubes
1/4 cup all purpose flour
1/2 cup milk or heavy creem
2/3 cup frozen peas

Instructions:
Step 1:
    Add 1 tablespoon olive oil to skillet over high heat. Add chicken and season with s&p, brown the meat/sear 5-6 minutes. Don't need to cook all the way through
    Move chicken to slow cooker when done.
Step 2:
    Add following to slow cooker: broth, wine, worcestershire, balsamic, thyme, rosemary, oregano, s&p. Stir til combined, then add garlic, onion, carrots and potatoes.
    Cover and cook on low for 7-8 hours or on high for 4-5 hours.
Step 3: 
    Remove 1/2 cup of chicken broth from the slow cooker and transfer to a bowl, then stir in milk and whisk in flow until no lumps remaining. Return to slow cooker
    and stir to combine. Stir in peas and cook uncovered on HIGH for 15-20 more minutes until chicken stew thickens up. Serve
